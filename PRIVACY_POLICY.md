# Privacy Policy for Foul & Fortune

**Effective Date:** January 5, 2026

**Foul & Fortune** ("we", "our", or "us") operates the **Foul & Fortune** mobile application (the "App").

## Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.** 

All game data, settings, statistics, and match history are stored **locally on your device**. We have no access to your data.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that collect data (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or advertising networks).

## Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## Be aware regarding "Data Safety"

Since we do not collect any data, our Google Play "Data Safety" declaration states that **no data is collected or shared**.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository or support email.
