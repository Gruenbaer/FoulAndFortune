# Foul & Fortune - Branding Guidelines

## Logo Analysis

The logo perfectly embodies the dual-theme concept:

### **Visual Elements**

1. **Cue Ball Center**
   - Classic white cue ball with red dots
   - Represents the core billiards focus
   - Clean, recognizable, universal

2. **Steampunk Elements**
   - Brass/bronze gears and cogs
   - Mechanical, vintage aesthetic
   - Warm metallic tones (gold, bronze, copper)
   - Industrial revolution era feel

3. **Cyberpunk Elements**
   - Neon green "FOUL &" text
   - Neon yellow "FORTUNE" text
   - Circuit board patterns in background
   - Electric blue traces
   - Pink/magenta accent lines
   - Digital, futuristic aesthetic

4. **Color Palette**
   - **Neon Green**: #39FF14 (FOUL &)
   - **Neon Yellow/Gold**: #FFD700 (FORTUNE)
   - **Electric Blue**: #00F0FF (circuits)
   - **Pink/Magenta**: #FF00FF (accent traces)
   - **Bronze/Brass**: #B5A642 (gears)
   - **Dark Navy**: #0A1020 (background)
   - **White**: #FFFFFF (cue ball)
   - **Red**: #FF0000 (ball dots)

### **Typography**
- Bold, outlined letters
- Neon glow effect
- Curved to follow circular logo
- High contrast against dark background

---

## Theme Integration

### **Existing Themes to Keep**

Both current themes align perfectly with the logo:

1. **Cyberpunk Theme** (Default)
   - Matches neon text and circuit board elements
   - Use logo's neon colors as primary palette
   - Circuit patterns for backgrounds

2. **Steampunk Theme** (Alternative)
   - Matches gear/cog elements
   - Use logo's brass/bronze tones
   - Mechanical textures for backgrounds

### **Unified Design Language**

The logo shows how to blend both themes:
- **Mechanical meets Digital**
- **Vintage meets Futuristic**
- **Warm metals meets Cool neons**

---

## App Icon Strategy

### **Primary App Icon**
Use the full circular logo as-is:
- Instantly recognizable
- Works at all sizes
- Unique in app stores
- Tells the brand story

### **Adaptive Icon (Android)**
- **Background**: Dark navy with circuit pattern
- **Foreground**: Cue ball + gears (simplified)
- **Monochrome**: Cue ball silhouette

### **Icon Sizes**
The logo's circular design scales perfectly:
- 1024x1024 (App Store)
- 512x512 (Play Store)
- 192x192 (Android launcher)
- 180x180 (iOS)
- 32x32 (Favicon)

## Conclusion

The "Foul & Fortune" branding perfectly captures the app's essence:
- **Foul**: The challenge, the mistakes, the learning
- **Fortune**: The success, the skill, the victory
- **Dual Theme**: Honoring tradition while embracing innovation

The logo is distinctive, scalable, and tells a story. It will stand out in app stores and create strong brand recognition.
